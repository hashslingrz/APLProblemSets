Creating the matrix

The "outer product" operator applies its function operand to all combinations of elements of its left and right argument arrays. 

      f←{⍺+⍵}

11 21 31
12 22 32
13 23 33

For example, the catenate function , (comma) will join two lists together. We can use the outer product to join combinations of words from two lists. 

      1 4 9 , 6 5 4
